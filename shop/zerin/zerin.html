Architecture

How it works

Everything runs in your browser. Data is stored in localStorage — no requests ever leave your device. The service worker caches all assets on first load, giving you full offline functionality.

Add it to your iOS or Android home screen and it behaves exactly like a native app: full-screen, no browser chrome, proper safe-area inset handling.

Export your data at any time as a standard CSV. Import from any bank export. You own everything completely.

Overview

OVW

Your financial snapshot — income, expenses, trends, and budget progress at a glance.

See total income, expenses, and net balance for any month. Navigate forward and backward between months freely.

Spot spending patterns immediately. The chart updates live as you add transactions — no setup required.

Hairline progress bars per category. Turns amber at 80% and red at 100% of your set budget limit.

Instantly know if you're spending more or less than last month without digging through numbers.

$budget --status

02

Ledger

LDG

Full transaction history with search, filtering, and instant export.

Type any keyword to instantly filter your transaction history. Results update as you type.

One-tap filter to isolate any transaction type. Combine with search for precise results.

Swipe a row left on mobile to reveal delete. Feels completely native.

Use existing categories or create your own. Emoji makes scanning your list fast and visual.

One tap exports every transaction in clean CSV compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, or any tool.

ledger

$ledger --export csv
[+] 247 transactions [OK] budget-export.csv ready

Recurring

REC

Templates for recurring income, expenses, and transfers. One tap to apply when due.

Set any item to repeat monthly or yearly. Works for income, expenses, and inter-account transfers.

Templates pre-fill all fields. Confirm the amount and tap apply — takes under two seconds.

Each template stores account, amount, category, and note — fully pre-filled on apply.

Import / Export

I/O

Bring in your bank data. Take your data anywhere. No lock-in, ever.

Download your bank's transaction export and import it directly. Date, amount, and description are parsed automatically.

The export includes date, type, amount, account, category, emoji, and note. Fully portable to any spreadsheet app.

No proprietary format. No account to delete. If you ever move on, your full data goes with you.

import-export

Compatibility

Runs everywhere.

On iOS, use Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen for the full native experience with offline support and no browser chrome.

On Android, Chrome will prompt you to install automatically. The service worker caches all assets so it loads instantly — even with no connection.
